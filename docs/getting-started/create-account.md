# Create a Stendly Account

## Step 1: Register

1. Navigate to [dashboard.stendly.com](https://dashboard.stendly.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address and create a strong password
4. Click **Create Account**

## Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your inbox for a verification code from Stendly
2. Verify your email
3. If you don't see it, check your spam folder

## Step 3: Complete KYB (Know Your Business)

To comply with regulations, you need to provide your business details:

1. **Business Name** — Your registered legal business name
2. **Website URL** — Your business website
3. **Support Email** — Customer support contact
4. **Business Description** — What your business does

Your application is typically reviewed within 1-2 business days.

## Step 4: Access the Dashboard

Once approved, you can access:

- **Dashboard** — Payment volume, transaction stats
- **API Keys** — Generate and manage API keys
- **Webhooks** — Configure webhook endpoints
- **Terminals** — Manage POS terminals
